1835 United Kingdom general election

dates
    The eleventh United Kingdom Parliament was dissolved on 29 December 1834
    The new Parliament was summoned to meet on 19 February 1835, for a maximum seven-year term from that date.
    The general election took place between January and February 1835. The first nomination was on 5 January, with the first contest on 6 January and the last contest on 6 February 1835.
