Alexandra Radovic

Alexandra is a journalist in the Capital News Service Annapolis bureau. She has worked as the Editor-In-Chief for Campus Current, an intern at College Park Here & Now and a reporter for The Washington Blade, County Sports Zone and Marymount Lacrosse. She also hosted a Q&A covered by The Capital Gazette and runs her own reporting-based Youtube Channel
